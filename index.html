Choose from our range of fursuit components

Head

Our heads are initially constructed from a 3D printed outline that is designed according to your reference sheet in 3D modeling software. We find that this makes it a lot easier to build up the foam foundation more accurately rather than starting from scratch with the foam. The usual features of our fursuit heads include hair, tongues, follow-me eyes, teeth, and ears. Of course, we have a lot more available listed on the right and if you'd like something more than what is listed here feel free to ask!
